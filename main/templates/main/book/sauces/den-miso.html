Miso Glaze

SWEET MISO GLAZE (JAPANESE DEN MISO)

Den miso is a Japanese sauce commonly used for glazing cod. But modern sushi restaurants have made this sauce really popular. Forever. Nigiri salmon glazed with this sauce will surely please even the discerning gourmet.

| cooking 10min |

INGREDIENTS

  • Miso paste - Shiro(white) miso - 200g  4/5cup
  • Mirin - 70ml 1/4cup
  • Sake - 70ml 1/4cup
  • Sugar - 100g 1/2cup
    •

    INSTRUCTIONS

    1 Put the sake and mirin in a nonreactive saucepan and bring to a boil.
    2 Add the miso paste and blend it into a smooth sauce.
    3 Add the sugar and continue stirring so the sauce does not burn.
    4 When the sugar dissolves, turn off the heat and cool to room temperature.

    Keep in the refrigerator for up to 1 months.
