Spicy Mayo

Author Ruslan Kovalev

Spicy mayonnaise is a rich & yummy sauce, incredibly popular in sushi restaurants and very easy to cook.

I will reveal for you one of the most delicious and special recipes, and other one, called "classic", in short description.

Flying fish caviar will give incredible texture, and yuzu juice will add super rich flavor.

| cooking 5min |

INGREDIENTS

  • Japanese Kewpie mayonnaise - 100ml
  • sriracha hot chili sauce - 20ml
    •
  • sweet chili sauce - 10ml
    •
  • Chili oil - 5ml
    •
  • Togarashi (Mixed Chili) - 3gr
    •
  • Yuzu juice - 5ml
    •
  • Sesame oil - 5ml
    •
  • Tobiko (flying fish roe) ~ 5gr
    •

    INSTRUCTIONS

    Combine ingredients and mix well.

    Another simple Spicy mayonnaise recipe is 1 cup mayonnaise, 4 tablespoons Sriracha sauce and 1 teaspoon sesame oil.
