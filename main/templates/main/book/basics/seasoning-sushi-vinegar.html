Sushi Vinegar

The reason sushi rice is so amazing is the balanced stimulation of most of the tongue receptors and, of course, the special texture. but how?!

Sweet and sour salty seasoning with umami flavor. This is what you need to make your receptors crazy! The only question is the ratio of the required components. And the solution is a special sushi rice seasoning! (Sushi vinegar).

Sushi vinegar can be cooked in advance and stored, or for one meal. the recipe for ease of conversion will be presented as 1 liter of vinegar. and if you cook for 1 meal, then for 1 cup of dry rice. This Cooking Method is an average version of popular sushi restaurants.

| cooking 5-10min |

| per liter of rice vinegar |

  • Japanese Rice vinegar - 1000ml
  • Sugar - 700g
    •
  • Salt or sea salt - 170g
    •
  • Kombu ~ 10x10cm square.
    •

    | per cup of dry rice |

  • Japanese Rice vinegar - 30ml
  • Sugar - 20g
    •
  • Salt or sea salt - 5g
    •
  • Kombu ~ 3x3cm square.

    Kombu - Dried Seaweed (Optional): gives the rice a traditional umami flavor
    •

    Mix together vinegar, salt, sugar and combo in a small pot, on medium heat (do not boil) until the salt and sugar are completely dissolved.
